About

#OOTD is a revolutionary fashion application designed to streamline your morning routine by helping you document, create, and share your daily outfits. It offers innovative features like outfit comparison and event-specific suggestions, ensuring your style is always on point. Additionally, it fosters collaborative decision-making, allowing friends to participate in shaping your unique fashion statement.

Designers: Hannah C, Hana D

Developers: Evan C, Caroline W.
